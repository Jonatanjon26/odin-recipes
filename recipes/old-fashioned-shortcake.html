Old-Fashioned Shortcake

Description

This easy recipe for shortcake was given to me by a friend. It was her mother's recipe, and it has become one of my favorite summer dessert recipes. These fabulous cakes sop up all the strawberry juices, and the touch of nutmeg sends them over the top!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup white sugar

  • 4 teaspoons baking powder

  • ½ teaspoon salt

  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg (Optional)

  • ½ cup cold butter, cubed

  • ⅓ cup milk

  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch baking pan.

  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg together in a large bowl.

  3. Cut in butter with 2 knives or a pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Beat together milk and egg in a small bowl

  5. stir into flour mixture until batter is just blended

  6. Spread batter in the prepared baking pan.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 15 minutes

  8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
